Design Language

Ethereal Portfolio Principles

A page distilled from the design document: editorial minimalism, intentional asymmetry, and depth without hard lines.

Creative North Star

The Silent Curator: use margin as structure, not empty space. Replace noise with rhythm and atmospheric pacing.

Tonal Palette

Sand, stone, and clay: warm paper backgrounds, muted graphite typography, restrained terracotta accents.

Do and Do Not

Do

Use spacing to define groups, stack tonal layers for depth, and keep interactions quiet and slow.

Do Not

Avoid hard black, avoid noisy dividers, and avoid crowded blocks that collapse visual breathing room.

Typography

Newsreader leads with editorial weight. Manrope carries body text with clarity and calm.

Depth

Prefer layered surfaces and ambient shadows over heavy borders or abrupt contrast jumps.

Implementation Note

This website applies those principles through one shared visual system across all pages, with cohesive navigation and measured motion.
